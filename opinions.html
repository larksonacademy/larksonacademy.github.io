Published Opinions

The Supreme Court publishes slip opinions that announce the high court's ruling on a case before it is published into a bound volume at the end of the year. These cases are cited as [Appellant] v. [Appellee], [TermYear] M.S.C. ___ (2023). Once they are published in a volume, they receive a different citation.
